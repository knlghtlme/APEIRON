Invest In All Kids!

This petition advocates for a more equal distribution of resources in the Chicago high schools. In the last decade, a majority of the Chicago Public School resources have been centered around the Selective Enrollment schools, making local neighborhood schools lose out on resources that could contribute to many students' education. Sign to help these kids reach their potential.
