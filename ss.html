Success Stories

Jet Nugyen

UPENN '28

Jet Nguyen, a first-generation, low-income student from Chicago, has always dreamt of attending a top-tier university. However, the lack of resources and guidance at his high school made this dream seem unattainable. That’s when Jet discovered Apeiron. Through Apeiron, Jet found a comprehensive support system that provided him with personalized college counseling, SAT prep resources, and financial aid workshops. Apeiron’s mentors helped Jet navigate the complex college application process, refine his essays, and prepare for interviews. Additionally, Apeiron connected Jet with scholarship opportunities that alleviated the financial burden of college. Jet’s hard work and determination paid off when he received an acceptance letter from the University of Pennsylvania. Now, as a proud member of the Class of 2028, Jet is pursuing a degree in Economics with a minor in Computer Science at the Wharton School. He’s passionate about using his education to give back to his community and support other first-generation students like himself. Jet’s journey from dreams to infinity exemplifies the transformative impact of Apeiron.

Raymond Chen

WASHU '28

Raymond Chen grew up in a low-income neighborhood in Chicago, where educational opportunities were limited. Despite the challenges, Raymond excelled academically and was determined to attend a prestigious university. However, the path to higher education seemed daunting without proper guidance and support. Raymond’s life changed when he joined Apeiron. With access to a wealth of resources, Raymond received expert advice on crafting his college applications, preparing for standardized tests, and securing financial aid. Apeiron’s community of mentors and peers provided the encouragement and support he needed to stay focused on his goals. Raymond’s dedication and the support from Apeiron led to his acceptance at Washington University in St. Louis. As a member of the incoming class, Raymond is excited to study Computer Engineering, with aspirations of contributing to technological advancements that can improve lives. His story is a testament to Apeiron’s mission of turning dreams into reality for first-generation, low-income students.

Justin Zhao

UIC '28

Justin Zhao, hailing from a working-class family in Chicago, faced numerous obstacles in his pursuit of higher education. As a first-generation student, Justin had to navigate the college application process on his own, with limited access to resources and support. Despite these challenges, Justin was determined to succeed. Apeiron became a crucial part of Justin’s journey. Through the program, Justin gained access to tailored guidance on selecting colleges, writing compelling personal statements, and applying for financial aid. The workshops and one-on-one mentoring sessions provided him with the knowledge and confidence to tackle each step of the application process. Justin’s perseverance and the comprehensive support from Apeiron culminated in his acceptance to the University of Illinois Chicago. There, he plans to major in Computer Science, aiming to develop innovative technologies that can make a difference in the world. Justin’s success story underscores the importance of Apeiron’s mission to empower first-generation, low-income students to reach their full potential and achieve their academic dreams.
